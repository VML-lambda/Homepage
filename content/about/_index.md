introHeading: What is Visual Media?
introBody: '**Visual media** refers to digital imagery that is visually consumed and utilized, encompassing **not only 2D content** delivered through TV, mobile devices, and the internet, **but also VR/AR footage and volumetric spatial imagery.**'
introChecklist:
  - UHD/8K/16K High-Definition 2D Video
  - VR/AR Video Using HMDs and Related Devices
  - Multi-view 3D, Light Field, and Holographic Volumetric Imagery
introBody2: The digital visual media has been expanding both in quality and quantity, and as it **converges with artificial intelligence to become increasingly intelligent and sophisticated**, its technologies and services are **spreading into other industries** such as autonomous driving, defense, healthcare, and education. Accordingly, the importance and impact of the visual media sector is expected to grow even further in the years ahead.
introHighlight: The core technologies driving visual media research include **"Image Processing"**, **"Machine Learning"**, and **"Computer Vision"**. Our lab aims to contribute to building a more immersive digital world through the **international standardization** of the technologies we develop.
