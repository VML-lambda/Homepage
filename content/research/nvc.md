We research **end-to-end optimized neural network-based image and video compression** technologies that go beyond traditional hand-crafted codecs (AVC, HEVC, VVC, etc.). By leveraging learned image coding, we aim to replace conventional hand-crafted approaches and achieve significantly more efficient compression of visual data.

### Key Approaches

- **Tool-based Approach**: Easily adaptable to existing codec architectures with straightforward extension to inter-coding.
- **End-to-End Approach**: A completely new design that demonstrates excellent performance in image compression, with ongoing research into video extension.
