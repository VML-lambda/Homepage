Holography is an ideal technology that reproduces the light of objects in space, eliminating the vergence-accommodation conflict inherent in conventional 3D displays. However, it requires massive data volumes and extremely high resolution.

We research **neural network-based hologram compression and generation** technologies to efficiently process vast holographic data.

### NHVC (Neural Hologram Video Coding)

A Phase Hologram Image/Video Compressor & Generator that achieves both efficient compression and high-quality generation of holographic video.
